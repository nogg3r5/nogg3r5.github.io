Ways I Like to Brew Cofee

Espresso

My favourite coffee is a cappuccino, with a little sugar, in a fairly small cup. Usually around 10-12 ounces. Compared to an espresso, this is fairly large. Compared to filter coffee, this is fairly small.

Usually I make this with an espresso machine, a double shot of coffee, topped with foamed milk and a little heart poured into the foam. A skill I recently taught myself.

I weigh out 20 grams of beans, grind them in a hand powered grinder and pour them into the portafilter. I use a distributor to make sure they're evenyl spread through the portafilter and then tamp them.

If I'm away, on holiday I often use a Minipresso as a standin for my espresso machine, its almost as good, although it only brews a single shot. I use a small electric whisk to make foamed milk.
