A Few Good things

Below I have assembled a list of things I like, whether they're good quality, fun to use or both.

  • Overcast

    My podcast player of choice. I use it to listen to a whole bunch of podcasts on my way to work. A few favourites:

    • 99% Invisible
    • ATP
    • Automators
    • The Allusionist
    • Cortex
    • Darknet Diaries
    • Dissect
    • Freakonomics Radio
    • Hello Internet
    • HiPhi Nation
    • HowSound
    • Liftoff
    • Playing for Fun
    • Reply All
    • Song Exploder
    • Top Four
    • Under the Radar
  • NewsBlur

    My favourite RSS Reader, I';ve used Newsblur since its launch and been a paid subscriber for almost as long. I subscribe to a whole list of websites via RSS from tech news to design, to male fashion.

  • Zojirushi Travel Mug

    My favourite way to keep tea warm for ages. My work office has a hot water tap that makes water that doesnt stay hot for long, this mug keeps my tea too hot to drink for longer than it needs to. 10/10

  • Drevo Bluetooth Mechanical Keyboard - Almost UK Layout

    Despite not being perfect, this is the only mechanical keyboard I could find that has both bluetooth and a UK/ISO layout. Even then, the ~ and # key is above a smaller enter key. At least | is in the correct place. Mechanical Keyboards can be a bit of a rabbit hole

  • Logitech MX Master

    Spending 8 hours a day at a computer means I want comfortable tools to work with. The MX Master and the Drevo mechanical keyboard make working all day a delight.
